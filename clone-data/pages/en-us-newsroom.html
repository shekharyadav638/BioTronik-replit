BIOTRONIK Announces First U.S. Implant of Investigational Next-Generation Leadless Pacemaker

BIOTRONIK, a global leader in cardiovascular medical technology, today announced the first U.S. implant of its LivIQ leadless pacemaker as part of the BIO-LivIQ global pivotal clinical trial. This next-generation single-device solution is designed to deliver atrioventricular (AV) synchrony through advanced electrical atrial far-field sensing.
